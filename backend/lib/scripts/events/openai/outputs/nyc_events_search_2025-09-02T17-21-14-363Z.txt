   A major music festival held at Randall’s Island in early July, featuring top-tier artists across diverse genres. A must-see for music lovers and a fixture of NYC’s summer scene. 3. **Summer Streets (expanded 400-block edition)**  
   Running July 26 to August 23, this year’s edition marked NYC’s 400th anniversary with over 400 car-free blocks across all five boroughs. It offered open-air entertainment, fitness events, art installations, and more—one of the largest iterations in the program’s history. ([secretnyc.co](https://secretnyc.co/things-to-do-in-august-nyc-events-2025/?utm_source=openai), [timeout.com](https://www.timeout.com/newyork/events-calendar/august-events-calendar?utm_source=openai))

4. **Jazz Age Lawn Party – 20th Anniversary**  
   On August 9–10, Governors Island hosted the annual Jazz Age Lawn Party, a glitzy Prohibition-era experience featuring live jazz, dance lessons, vintage cars, retro cocktails, and 1920s-themed crowds—celebrating two decades of stylish summer revelry. ([secretnyc.co](https://secretnyc.co/things-to-do-in-august-nyc-events-2025/?utm_source=openai))

5. **2025 US Open Tennis Tournament**  
   The Grand Slam event began on **August 26** at the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center in Queens. It features world-class tennis, celebrity appearances, and key induction ceremonies—such as for the Bryan Brothers and Maria Sharapova.
